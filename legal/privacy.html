Privacy

Effective July 3, 2026

Memento is built local-first. Your goals, answers, notes, reflections, and images live on your device, not on our servers. This page lists, plainly, the few things that ever leave it.

What stays on your device

Everything you write. Your Clarity answers, your goal, your daily moves, notes, reflections, streaks, and check-ins are stored in your browser's local storage. Vision-board and journal images never leave your device under any circumstances, not even with sync on. No account is required to use Memento, and clearing your browser data erases it all (use the backup button in your Profile first).

What leaves your device, and why

  • AI features. When you use an AI feature (like Clarity discovery or the coach), the text needed for that feature is sent through our server to Anthropic, the company behind the Claude models, to generate the response. Anthropic does not train its models on this data. We do not store your AI conversations on our servers.
  • Anonymous usage signals. We record which screens get used (events like "app opened" or "action completed") tied to a random device id, never to your name, email, or anything you write. This tells us what to improve. No third-party analytics, no advertising trackers, no fingerprinting.
  • Reminders, if you turn them on. To send push notifications we store your device's push address, timezone offset, the name of your current daily move, and whether today is done. Turning notifications off in your system settings expires the subscription and we delete the record.
  • Sync, if you create an account. Signing in (magic link or Google) mirrors your Memento data to our database so you can pick it up on another device. It is protected so only your account can read it. Images still never sync.
  • Payments. Purchases are handled by our payment provider. We never see or store your card number.

What we never do

We do not sell your data. We do not show ads. We do not share anything with data brokers. There is nothing to sell: the product is the one-time purchase, not you.

Deleting your data

"Reset Everything" in your Profile wipes your device copy. If you made an account or turned on reminders and want the server-side records gone too, email us and we will delete them.

Changes

If this policy changes in a way that matters, we will say so in the app, not bury it here.
